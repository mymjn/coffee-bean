History of Coffee

Before 1400 - 1500

Before 1400

According to legend, Kaldi the shepherd discovered coffee.

1400

Coffee trees were growing in Yemeni, Arabia.

1500

Coffee was a popular beverage in Persia, Syria, Turkey, and Egypt.

1600 - 1700

1600

Coffee reaches Europe.

1615

Pope Clement VIII declares coffee is not the work of Satan.

1650

More than 300 coffee shops open in London.Coffee comes to America.

1675

The Dutch obtain seedlings to grow new coffee trees.

1700 -

1714

King Louis XIV of France received a gift of a coffee tree from Amsterdam.

1773

America chooses coffee as their favorite beverage.

1800

More than 18 million trees are growing from a single seedling obtained from the tree of King Louis XIV of France.

Popular Coffee Flavors

Pumpkin Spice

Pumpkin spice coffee has a warm, spiced flavor, typically with notes of cinnamon, nutmeg, and clove.

Mocha

Mocha is a type of coffee beverage that combines espresso, chocolate, and milk. It is typically made with espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup or cocoa powder, which gives it a rich, chocolatey flavor.

Hazelnut

Hazelnut coffee is a flavored coffee with a nutty, slightly sweet flavor with notes of hazelnuts.

Cinnamon

Cinnamon coffee is an arranged coffee with cinnamon added to the coffee. It is characterized by the spicy flavor of cinnamon that is added to coffee to create a unique taste.
